🚇 How to Get to the Wedding Venue

From: Gyeongbokgung Station (Line 3) → King Sejong Memorial Hall

Recommended Route (Subway + Shuttle or Walking):

  1. Take Subway Line 3 northbound to Yaksu Station, transfer to Line 6.
  2. Get off at Korea University Station Exit 3.

Options to Venue:
• Free shuttle bus every 15 minutes from Exit 3 🚐
• Walk 750m (10–15 mins) 🚶

Taxi: ~₩10,000–15,000 from central Seoul.

✈️ Korea Travel Tips ✈️

1. Must-Have Apps

  • Naver Maps, KakaoMap, KakaoMetro
  • Korea Tour Card (T-Money)
  • KakaoT (Taxi)

2. Subway & Public Transport

Efficient, English announcements, use T-Money card (₩1,350 per ride).

3. Airport to City

  • AREX Express: Non-stop, 43 mins, ₩9,500.
  • AREX All-Stop: 58 mins, ₩4,150.
  • Limousine Bus: ₩10,000–17,000, 60–75 mins.

4. Vegetarian & Vegan Restaurants

See list in the document for recommended spots in Jongno, Hongdae, Gangnam, Itaewon.

5. Money & Payments

Cards widely accepted; carry ~€200 in cash for small vendors and snacks.

6. Intercity Travel

KTX high-speed trains and express buses connect major cities. Book online in advance.

7. Flights to Jeju

Frequent flights (~every 15 mins), 1 hour, multiple carriers. Depart from GMP or ICN.

8. Cultural Tips & Etiquette

  • Bow slightly, remove shoes indoors, no tipping, carry trash with you, subway etiquette.

9. SIM Card / Wi-Fi

Rent pocket Wi-Fi or buy SIM at airport; free Wi-Fi in cafés and stations.

10. Emergency & Safety

  • 112 – Police
  • 119 – Fire & Ambulance
  • 1330 – Korea Travel Hotline (English)
